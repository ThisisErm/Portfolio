About

My name is Erm

I'm a Software Engineer and Full-Stack Web Developer based in NYC. My passion for computers began when I was just five years old in La Romana, Dominican Republic, when I first laid my hands on a keyboard. At the time, my mother was taking a typing class for work and brought me along with her in the evenings. What started with playing solitaire & browsing Cartoon Network, turned into building gaming PCs, work stations, home servers, and virtual machines. This lead me to pursue a career in Systems Administration. My curiosity and desire to learn what makes computers and systems tick, eventually lead me to pursue a career as a Software Engineer, which I find incredibly rewarding.
